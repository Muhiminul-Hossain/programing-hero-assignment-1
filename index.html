Frequently Asked Question

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf

What is included in the ticket price? +
Your ticket includes access to all sessions, workshops, networking events, meals during the conference days, and a digital copy of all speaker slides after the event.
Can I upgrade my plan later? +
Yes. You can upgrade from Standard to Pro or Team anytime before the event starts. Just contact support and pay the price difference.
Will sessions be recorded? +
All main stage sessions will be recorded and shared with Pro and Team ticket holders within a week after the conference ends.
Is there a discount for students? +
Yes, students get 30% off any ticket type with a valid student ID uploaded during checkout.
How do I get a refund if I cannot attend? +
Refunds are available up to 14 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your ticket to someone else free of charge.
